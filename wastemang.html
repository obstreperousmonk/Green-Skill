Introduction

Waste management involves the collection, transport, processing, recycling, and disposal of waste materials. It aims to reduce the adverse effects of waste on human health and the environment.

Types of Waste

  • Municipal Solid Waste
  • Hazardous Waste
  • Industrial Waste
  • Biomedical Waste

Waste Management Processes

  • Recycling
  • Composting
  • Incineration
  • Landfilling

Importance of Waste Management

Effective waste management is crucial for maintaining a clean environment, preventing pollution, conserving resources, and protecting public health.

Tips for Reducing Waste

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Compost organic waste
  • Donate items instead of discarding
